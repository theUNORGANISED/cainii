The Problem

Nonprofits are running
on duct tape and spreadsheets.

The tools that exist are built for sales teams or cost $500/month. Neither works for organizations operating on tight budgets to do meaningful work.

💸

Salesforce is $150/user/month

Enterprise CRMs are built for revenue teams. Nonprofits get a stripped-down edition that still costs more than their entire software budget.

📋

Excel doesn't scale

Most small NGOs manage hundreds of donors in spreadsheets. No history, no automation, no collaboration — just a file that breaks when two people edit it.

🔌

Nothing talks to each other

Grant management is one app. Outreach is another. Impact reporting is a Word doc. Hours disappear copying data between systems that don't connect.

The Solution

One tool. Built for the
mission, not the margin.

Cainii connects your donors, grants, volunteers, and impact data in one place — at a price nonprofits can actually afford.

👥

Donor & Relationship Management

Track every interaction with every donor, foundation, and partner. Full communication history, giving history, relationship health at a glance.

🎯

Grant Pipeline Tracking

Never miss a deadline. Track grants from discovery to reporting with deadline reminders, document storage, and reporting templates built in.

📊

Impact Reporting Dashboard

Turn program data into compelling stories for donors and boards. Automated reports, one-click data exports, visual dashboards.

✉️

Outreach & Campaign Tools

Run donor campaigns, send targeted newsletters, track open rates — all without leaving your CRM. No third-party tools needed.
