The Founder

We are passionate about giving nonprofits the tools they actually deserve.

Every morning at the charity I worked at started the same way — manually pulling donation data from PayPal and the bank into a spreadsheet before I could do anything useful.

Thank-you emails went out by hand. We had no idea which campaigns were working, who had donated before, or where new donors were finding us. Hours vanished into admin that should have taken minutes.

Cainii is the tool I needed then. Built from that experience, shaped by conversations with NGOs who are living the same problem right now.
