The Problem

Nonprofits are running
on duct tape and spreadsheets.

The CRMs that exist are either built for sales teams or cost $500/month. Neither option works for organizations operating on tight budgets to do meaningful work.

💸

Salesforce is $150/user/month

Enterprise CRMs are built for revenue teams. Nonprofits get a stripped-down "nonprofit edition" that still costs more than their software budget.

📋

Excel doesn't scale

Most small NGOs manage hundreds of donors in spreadsheets. There's no history, no automation, no collaboration — just a file that breaks when two people edit it.

🔌

Tools don't talk to each other

Grant management is one app. Donor outreach is another. Impact reporting is a Word doc. Nothing is connected, so hours are lost to copying data between systems.

The Solution

One tool. Built for the mission,
not the margin.

CommonGood CRM connects your donors, grants, volunteers, and impact data in one place — at a price nonprofits can actually afford.

👥

Donor & Relationship Management

Track every interaction with every donor, foundation, and partner. See the full picture: communication history, giving history, relationship health score.

🎯

Grant Pipeline Tracking

Never miss a deadline again. Track grants from discovery to reporting with deadline reminders, document storage, and reporting templates built in.

📊

Impact Reporting Dashboard

Turn your program data into compelling stories for donors and boards. Automated annual reports, one-click data exports, visual dashboards.

✉️

Outreach & Campaign Tools

Run donor campaigns, send targeted newsletters, and track open rates — all without leaving your CRM. No Mailchimp needed.
